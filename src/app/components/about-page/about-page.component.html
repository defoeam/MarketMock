About MarketMock

MarketMock is an educational tool meant to help aspiring investors get aquainted with the stock market. MarketMock provides a simulated environment that replicates real-world trading conditions using real stock market data, allowing users to experiment with different investment strategies, analyze market trends, and learn from their successes and failures without the risk of losing real money. By using MarketMock, aspiring investors can gain the knowledge and confidence they need to navigate the stock market successfully.
